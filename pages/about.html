About the Author

I capture worlds and adventures from daydreams, turn them into stories, and share them to avid explorers through text.

I started imagining tales before I could put them into words. I'm an avid fan of mystery so while I don't stick with one particular literary genre in my work, there's always bound to be enigmas and plot twists in all of them.

I'm also a proud mother of three dogs. When I'm not immersed in storytelling, I dabble in art and music, experiment in the kitchen, and develop my knitting skills.
