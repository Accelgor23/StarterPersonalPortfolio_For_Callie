Books

  • Lunar Frost

    Aurel Cassander, a brilliant nutrition scientist, pursues solace in teaching after surviving a tragedy in his first deployment to a lunar base facility. Eirwen Gannon, a mysterious engineer, suddenly meets him, stirring echoes of his painful past. Summoned back to the moon, Aurel faces unusual circumstances and dangerous incidents alongside Eirwen. Together, they must untangle the web of secrets shrouding the lunar base and Aurel's haunting past.

    What hidden truths will they find once the sheets of lunar ice melts?

  • Lahnthean Aria

    When Kiran rescues campus royalty Alessa during an earthquake, she tumbles into an abyss and wakes up to a foreign land, a traveler named Noorh, and his diamond blade ready to slice her throat open. She learns that she unwittingly took Alessa's place as the summoned Lahnthean Aria when she saved her. Together, they taken on a journey to rectify the error as Kiran temporarily assumes the role of Lahnthea's awaited savior. She unravels the secrets behind the title, Noorh's identity, and the magical and perilous world she was forced to live in. With each revelation comes a price - one that could cost Kiran her life.

    Will she be able to find her way home before it's too late?

  • Stranded

    On a mysterious island bridging worlds, Geale, a merman, is swept into a whirlwind romance with Marnthe, a captivating bar singer, amidst a mystical celebration. Desperate to prolong their fleeting connection, Geale chases after Marnthe which unwittingly leads to an accident. It leaves them stranded on the island together with Geale struggling to understand the enigmatic world of humans as he passionately pursues Marnthe's affection. He soon unravels the mystery behind Marnthe's identity along with the curse that has left him broken and afraid of falling in love.

    Will Geale's devotion be enough to save Marnthe? And will Marnthe survive Geale's chaotic misadventures?

  • The Taming Author

    Dean Trevor's dream promotion turns into a daunting task when he's assigned to edit the works of his literary idol, Claus Lorewind. The catch? Lorewind is actually a woman - an undoubtedly crazy one. Dean must navigate the challenges of keeping her on track while concealing her identity, keeping up with her quirks, and dealing with his growing feelings for her. Dean's loyalty to his job and his admiration for Lorewind's works are put to the test.

    How far will Dean go to keep his career, meet his deadlines, and finally tame the author?
